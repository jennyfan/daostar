A DAO is people, and a DAO is code.

DAOs represent one possible future for the internet. But that future is fragile. Use-cases are still being imagined. Business models have yet to be tested. And for all our collective wealth, it only takes one hedge fund manager to outbid us. We are far, far from disrupting the deathstars of Web2.

Our greatest strength is our ability to act, collectively. That’s why we’ve built the DAOstar standard. Imagine if you could use Aragon contracts in a Moloch DAO, or Gnosis Zodiac on top of Compound Governor Bravo. By interoperating with each other (and with Web2), we can realize the promise of Web3 for all communities on the internet.
